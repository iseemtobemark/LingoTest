In the list of protection schemes, you can:

Display the list of protections schemes per name, tenant and use.

Create, edit, delete and duplicate protection schemes.

Copy protection schemes to another tenant. To copy a protection scheme to another tenant:

Select the protection scheme, then click the button shaped like a globe.

Export a list of protection schemes in .CSV or .HTML formats.
