Duplication

In addition to mirroring and replication, you can also secure your data by performing duplication of your backups using the . See the for details.

On the other hand, the duplication of VLS or physical tapes to an is not supported. The duplication appears to be successful, no logs are generated but restoring the duplicated data will fail.

Direct SAN or library access:

If the is a Storage Node (i.e., it is connected to a SAN or to a tape library, and you want to duplicate data directly from the to the SAN or tape library) you must set the tunable dup_select_sn_for_hss to the name of the storage node on the Server. Otherwise, the duplication will go through the Server via the LAN, and performances will be degraded.
