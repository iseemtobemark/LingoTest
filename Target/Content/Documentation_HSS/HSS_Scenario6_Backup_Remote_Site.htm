Scenario 6 - Remote Sites backing up locally and replicating to the Central Site

The diagram (see Figure) illustrates the local backup process of remote sites and their replication to the central site.

Scenario 6

Securing the environment is managed by:

Replicating remote office (s) to the central site to prevent data loss

Remote s are also protected by stopping the and taking a snapshot.

Central is secured by mirroring to another (this is known as failover).

Central is backed up through script and snapshot (which is supported by the disk array) to prevent corruption.
